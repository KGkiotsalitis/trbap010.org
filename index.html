About Us

Welcome to the website of TRB's Transit Management and Performance Standing Committee. The AP010 committee is concerned with the relationship between transit management actions and performance through the analysis and development of management techniques, identification of appropriate performance measures and their application for improvement of information for transit management decision-making.
