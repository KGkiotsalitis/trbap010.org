Short Bio.

I am Assistant Professor in data science in transportation engineering at the University of Twente. From 2012-2015 I was Research Associate and from 2015-2018 Research Scientist at NEC Laboratories Europe. I am currently teaching 4 MSc courses, including Public Transport Modelling which is my main field of research.

Research

My research interests include public transport modelling, operations research, and mobility demand prediction. I am interested in utilizing our infrastructure and resources as efficiently as possible to improve mobility and accessibility.
